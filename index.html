Urban Gardening

Why urban gardening?

What is Urban Gardening?

Urban gardening is the practice of growing plants in urban areas, often in small spaces such as rooftops, balconies, or community gardens.

Benefits of Urban Gardening

Urban gardening has many benefits, including:

  • Increased access to fresh produce
  • Improved air quality
  • Reduced stress
  • Increased community involvement

How to Start an Urban Garden

If you're interested in starting an urban garden, there are a few things you'll need to do:

  1. Choose a location
  2. Prepare the soil
  3. Select plants
  4. Water and fertilize regularly
